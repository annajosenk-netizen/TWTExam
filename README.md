# Introduction
Welcome to the Quick Start Guide for updating WhatsApp Status. This document explains how to update and manage your WhatsApp statuses.
WhatsApp allows you to share multimedia content in your status that disappears in 24 hours. You can update your status from an iPhone or an Android phone.

## Prerequisites
To update a status, you need,
- A smartphone with internet connectivity.

# Updating your Status
WhatsApp allows you to update your status by either adding one or more statuses to your existing status sequence or deleting your current status. 
You can update your WhatsApp status with the following types of content. 
- Video
- Photo
- Text
- Voice
## Updating Status from an Android Phone
You can update your status from an Android phone by adding statuses to your existing status.
### Updating Status by adding a new Video
You can update your status by adding a video. You can either record a live video or select a video from your device to add to your status.

To update status,
1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your current status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.
The screen shows **Add Status**, if your status is empty.
2. On the screen, tap **My status**. This displays a list of your current active statuses.
3. Tap <img src="pen.png" width="15" height="15"> at the bottom of the screen. This opens a new screen with update options.
4. Tap **Video**.
5. You can add a video in one of the following ways.
    - Hold and press <img src="video.png" width="15" height="15"/>to record a live video. 
    
      (Optional) Tap  <img src="flip.png" width="15" height="15"/> to flip your camera before recording.
    - Tap <img src="gallery.png" width="15" height="15"/> to select a video from the device.
6. Customize the video using the following options.
    - Tap  <img src="music.png" width="15" height="15"/>    to add music.
    - Tap   <img src="stickers.png" width="15" height="15"/>   to add stickers.
    - Tap **Aa** to add text.You can change the text color, font, and alignment. 
    - Tap <img src="pen.png" width="15" height="15"/> to draw. You can change the line color.
    - Hold and drag the slider to adjust the start and end points.
7. On the bottom pane, tap @ in the text field to select the contacts whom you want to mention in this status.
8. On the bottom pane, tap Status (Contacts) if you want to modify who can view your status.
9. Tap <img src="send.png" width="15" height="15"/>.

This updates your status by adding a new video.

This adds the new video to the end of your current active status sequence.

### Updating Status by deleting your Status 

You can update your status by removing your status from an Android phone or iPhone. WhatsApp automatically removes the status updates after 24 hours.

To delete your status,

1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.  
2. On the screen, tap **My Status**. This displays a list of your active status updates.  
3. From the list, tap  <img src="threedots.png" width="15" height="15"/>   next to the status that you want to remove. This displays a list of options.  
4. From the list, tap **Delete**. A dialog box opens that asks your confirmation to delete.  
5. Click **Delete**.

This removes your status.

# Managing Status Privacy 
Your status is visible to all your contacts. WhatsApp allows you to decide who can see your status. The following options are available to manage your status privacy.

* Making your status visible to all your contacts  
* Hiding your status from some contacts  
* Making your status visible to some contacts  
* Allow sharing of your status  
* Sharing your status to Facebook and Instagram
## Making your status visible to all your contacts 

When you add or update your status, by default all your contacts can view it.   

To make your status visible to all your contacts,

1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.  
2. On the screen, tap <img src="threedots.png" width="15" height="15"/>  . This shows a list of options.  
3. From the list, select **Status Privacy**. This displays status privacy options.  
4. Tap **My** **contacts** to let all your contacts see your status.  
   

Your status updates are now visible to all your contacts.

## Hiding your status from some contacts 

You can hide your status from some contacts. You can hide either by clicking **Status(Contacts)** on the screen while adding or updating your status or from **Status Privacy**.  

To hide your status,

1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.  
2. On the screen, tap <img src="threedots.png" width="15" height="15"/>  . This shows a list of options.  
3. From the list, select **Status Privacy**. This displays status privacy options.  
4. Tap **My contacts except...** to hide your status from some contacts.   
5. Tap **0 excluded** to select the contacts from whom you want to hide your status.  
   

This hides your status from the selected contacts.

## Making your status visible to some contacts 

You can allow some contacts to view your status. You can perform this either by clicking **Status(Contacts)** on the screen while adding or updating your status or from **Status Privacy**.  

To make your status visible to some contacts,

1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.  
2. On the screen, tap <img src="threedots.png" width="15" height="15"/>  .This shows a list of options.  
3. From the list, select **Status Privacy**. This displays status privacy options.  
4. Tap **Only share with…** to share your status with selected contacts.   
5. Tap **0 included** to select the contacts with whom you want to share your status.  
   

Your status is now visible only to the selected contacts.

# Viewing your Status Updates 

When you update your status, your status is visible to your contacts.

To view your status updates,

1. On the bottom pane, tap **Updates**. This shows your current status and the status updates of your contacts on the top of the screen.  

   The green border around your profile photo in the status indicates a status update.
    
3. On the screen, tap **My status**. This displays a list of your current active statuses.  
4. From the list, tap on the status to view.

This displays your status updates in chronological order.
